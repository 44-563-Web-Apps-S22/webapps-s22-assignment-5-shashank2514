northeastern African bird

A drab sparrow of northeastern Africa. The head and underparts are gray, the wing and back are brown, and the rump is rufous. Found in dry thorn savanna and woodland, sometimes around human habitation

Powerful bird

Saker falcons are large, powerful birds of prey with an exceptionally broad wingspan. They have variable plumage ranging from chocolate brown color to a pale sandy with brown bars or streaks and can be almost pure white.
